Immediate Actions

When production fails, you must follow this timeline:

0-2 minutes: You should acknowledge the incident and notify many stakeholders
2-5 minutes: You can check logs and identify some error patterns
5-10 minutes: You must decide on rollback vs. hotfix
10+ minutes: You should implement the solution and monitor recovery

Survival Rules

Critical guidelines you must follow during emergencies:

  • You must stay calm - panic leads to many mistakes
  • You should communicate frequently with a lot of transparency
  • You can't skip testing even in emergencies
  • You must document some key decisions for post-mortem
  • You should have much coffee ready for long nights

Critical Warnings

Things you mustn't do during deployment crises:

  • You mustn't push untested code to production
  • You shouldn't make too many changes at once
  • You can't ignore some warning signs in monitoring
  • You mustn't forget to backup before any major changes
  • You shouldn't work alone on critical fixes
